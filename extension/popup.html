Import captions from YouTube

This extension reads the captions of a YouTube video you have open and sends them into Shadowing Space so you can practice shadowing.

  1. Open a YouTube video (any youtube.com/watch page).
  2. Click this toolbar icon again.
  3. Pick a caption track, then send or download it.

Open a specific video first

You are on YouTube, but not on a video page. Pick any video (its URL will look like youtube.com/watch?v=…), then click this icon again.
